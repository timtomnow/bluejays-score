The app follows the Toronto Blue Jays by default, but you can point it at any of the 30 MLB teams. Your pick is saved on this device and re-colors the whole app to match.

## Steps

1. Tap the **settings gear** at the bottom of the screen, next to the copyright line.
2. In the **Team** section, open the dropdown (it shows the team you're currently following).
3. Choose a team from the list.
4. Tap the **✕** to close Settings.

## What you'll see

The app updates right away — the header logo, team name, and colors switch to your chosen team, and the game card, season record, and standings now follow them. The standings also switch to that team's division. Your choice sticks the next time you open the app on this device.
