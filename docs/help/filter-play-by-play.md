The Play-by-Play tab lists every play in a game. Filters let you jump straight to hits, strikeouts, walks, or scoring plays — or a single inning — instead of scrolling the whole feed.

## Steps

1. Open a game's **Game Details** view and tap the **Play-by-Play** tab.
2. Tap a type pill to filter the feed: **All**, **Hits**, **Strikeouts**, **Walks**, or **Scoring**.
3. Under the inning pills, tap one or more inning numbers to limit the feed to those innings.
4. Tap an active pill again to turn it off and widen the feed.

## What you'll see

The play list updates immediately to match your filters — for example, **Scoring** in the **7th** shows only seventh-inning scoring plays. Home runs and scoring plays are emphasized, and each play is tagged with the team that made it.
