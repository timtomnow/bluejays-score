Blue Jays Score can live on your home screen and open full-screen, without the browser bars. It installs straight from your phone's browser — there's no app store download.

## Before you start

- Open the app in your phone's browser first (Safari on iPhone, Chrome on Android).

## Steps

### On iPhone (Safari)

1. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an up arrow) in the toolbar.
2. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
3. Tap **Add** in the top corner.

### On Android (Chrome)

1. Tap the **⋮** menu in the top corner.
2. Tap **Add to Home screen** (or **Install app** if it's offered).
3. Confirm by tapping **Add** or **Install**.

## What you'll see

A Blue Jays Score icon appears on your home screen. Opening it launches the app full-screen in the team's colors, just like a native app.
