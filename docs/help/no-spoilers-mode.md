No-spoilers mode hides every score on the home screen so you can check whether a game is on — and how far along it is — without finding out who's winning. The game time and the live inning still show; the scores stay blurred until you tap to reveal them. The setting is saved on this device and stays off until you turn it on.

## Steps

1. Tap the **settings gear** at the bottom of the screen, next to the copyright line.
2. In the **Viewing** section, turn **No-spoilers mode** on.
3. Tap the **✕** to close Settings.
4. Back on the home screen, tap any blurred score to reveal the scores for that game.

## What you'll see

With the mode on, the game card still shows the status (Live, Final, or the start time) and, for a live game, which half-inning it's in — for example **▲ 6th** for the top of the 6th or **▼ 6th** for the bottom. The actual numbers are blurred: the main score, the line score, the final game decisions, and the results in the **Last 5 Games** list.

Tap any blurred score and that card's scores come into focus at once. Other cards stay hidden until you tap them too. Turn the toggle back off in Settings to show every score again right away.
