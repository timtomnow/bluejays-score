Every live, finished, or recent game has a full **Game Details** view with player stats and the complete play-by-play. This guide shows the ways to open it.

## Steps

1. From the top game card, expand **Player Stats** or **Recent Plays**, or tap **See all plays →**.
2. From the **Last 5 Games** list, tap any result row.
3. From **Other MLB Games Today**, tap any game row.
4. The **Game Details** sheet slides up with two tabs: **Player Stats** and **Play-by-Play**.
5. Tap the **✕** to close the sheet when you're done.

## What you'll see

A bottom sheet titled with the game's matchup and date, showing the selected game. Use the tabs to switch between player stats and the play-by-play feed.
