Beyond the team you follow, Blue Jays Score lists all of the day's other MLB games so you can keep an eye on the rest of the league.

## Steps

1. Scroll to the **Other MLB Games Today** section near the bottom of the screen.
2. Read each row for the two teams, the score, and the game's state.
3. Tap any game row to open its full **Game Details** box score.

## What you'll see

The full slate of the day's games outside your team's. Tapping a game opens the same Game Details sheet — player stats and play-by-play — used for the Blue Jays' game.
