Below the season card, Blue Jays Score lists how the team has done lately and what's next on the schedule, so you can catch up at a glance.

## Steps

1. Scroll down to the **Last 5 Games** list. Each row shows the opponent, the date, and a colored **W** or **L** result with the final score.
2. Tap any row in **Last 5 Games** to open that game's box score.
3. Keep scrolling to **Upcoming Games** for the next scheduled matchups, their dates, and the probable starters.

## What you'll see

A compact history and schedule for the team you follow. Tapping a recent result opens the full box score for that game on the **Player Stats** tab.
