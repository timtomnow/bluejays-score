The Season card sums up how the team's year is going and shows the division race around them.

## Steps

1. Scroll to the **Season** card (labeled with the current year).
2. Read the large **record** and the colored **streak** pill (a win or loss streak) beside it.
3. Look below at the **division standings** table — it's headed by the team's division (**AL East** for the Blue Jays).
4. Find the team's row, highlighted in the team's accent color, to read its wins (**W**), losses (**L**), games behind (**GB**), and streak (**Str**).

## What you'll see

A quick read on the team's season: overall record, current streak, and standing within its division. If you switch teams, the standings switch to that team's division automatically.
