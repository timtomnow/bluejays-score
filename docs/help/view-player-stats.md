The Player Stats view breaks a game down to each player — the batting line and the pitching line — for both teams.

## Steps

1. Open a game's **Game Details** view and make sure the **Player Stats** tab is selected.
2. Use the two **team buttons** at the top to switch between the away and home team.
3. Read the **batting** table: at-bats, runs, hits, RBI, walks, and strikeouts, with a totals row at the bottom.
4. Scroll to the **Pitching** table for innings pitched, hits, earned runs, walks, and strikeouts.

## What you'll see

A full box score for the selected team. Tapping the other team button re-renders the batting and pitching tables for that side.
